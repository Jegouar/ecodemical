About ecodemical

Specialising in environmental education and sustainable solutions, Ecodemical works towards a more ecologically minded society and more empowered schools and businesses capable to meet the challenges of the present and future.

Drawing on over a decade’s worth of research into the science, politics and economics of climate change and sustainability and the social impacts of environmental challenges.

Research lead academic approach that can be tailored to suit you and your organisation, we will work with you to unlock your full sustainable potential and discover new opportunities.
